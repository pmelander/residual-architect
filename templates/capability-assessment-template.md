## Appendix: Maturity Level Definitions

### Level 1: Ad-hoc
- Inconsistent, reactive, individual-dependent
- "We figure it out as we go"
- High variability, repeated mistakes

### Level 2: Aware
- Aware of gaps, starting to improve
- "We know we should do this better"
- Inconsistent application

### Level 3: Defined
- Documented processes, consistent practices
- "This is how we do things here"
- Repeatable and reliable

### Level 4: Managed
- Metrics-driven, continuous measurement
- "We measure and optimize continuously"
- Data-informed improvement

### Level 5: Optimizing
- Continuous innovation, industry-leading
- "We're always getting better and teaching others"
- Exceptional performance
